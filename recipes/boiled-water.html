Lasagna!

Description

This is boiling water.

Do not drink, it will hurt you!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • (Optional) Salt
  • Just Water
  • Really, only water

Steps

  1. Fill a metal pot 2/3rd full of water
  2. Turn Burner to highest setting
  3. Wait until water is boiling. Add salt if desired
  4. Don't forget to turn off the burner when complete
