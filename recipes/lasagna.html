Lasagna!

Description

This is lasagna, it may not look like much, but it is yummy.

Guaranteed you will love it!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Red Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 degree F(190 degree C)
  2. In a large pot cook lasagna noodles in boiling salted water until al dente. Rinse with cool water. Drain.
  3. Cook Ground Beef
  4. Assemble ingredients
  5. Back at 375 for 30 minutes
